Example Diagrams

Below you see an example culture, represented as a box. There will be 40 such cultures displayed in each diagram. When you press the button labeled "Luminesce" the culture will luminesce briefly.

To the right of the culture is a circular indicator that indicates when radiation is applied. When you press the button labeled "Irradiate Me!," it will send out radiation. Please note this particular blue radiation has no effect while radiation during the experiment may or may not alter the rate of luminescence. Note also that in the experiment, the scientists will control when irratiation occurs, so there will be no "Irradiate Me!" button.
