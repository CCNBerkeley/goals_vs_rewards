Experiment Description

This experiment consists of a series of trials. In each trial, you'll first see a plus sign (+) and then choose an item. The plus only exists to serve as a visual aid for the location of the choices you'll be presented. When you've chosen an item will remain on the screen for a moment and then you will be presented with two boxes. You will have 1.5 seconds to choose one of the boxes, which will then remain on the screen for a moment before showing its contents. Shortly thereafter the next trial will begin.

Throughout the experiment, the 'F' key will choose items on the left, and the 'J' key will choose items on the right. Please note that a demonstration containing textual instructions reiterating those above, which closely resembles the real experiment, will follow this page, but that the real experiment will not have this text.

Click the "Play Example Trial" button when you are comfortable with the explanation above.
